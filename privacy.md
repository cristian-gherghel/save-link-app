# SaveLink App Extension – Privacy Policy

This extension is designed to help users save and organize web links in their own SaveLink App account.

**Data we collect**
- The URL and title of the page you choose to save.

**How data is used**
- The collected data is sent securely to your own SaveLink App account.
- We do not collect browsing history, track user behavior, or share data with third parties.

**Cookies**
- The extension may use a first-party session cookie on https://savelink.app/ to verify your login state.
- No third-party cookies are accessed or stored.

**Your control**
- Data is only transmitted when you explicitly click “Save.”
- You may remove all saved data at any time from your SaveLink App account.

If you have questions, contact support via [https://github.com/cristian-gherghel/save-link-app/issues](https://github.com/cristian-gherghel/save-link-app/issues).
